## Iteration 18 — US-018: Build trading sim hook (useTradingSim.ts)
- Created `useTradingSim.ts` with full tick-based trading simulation logic
- Price ticks simulated at 200ms intervals via setInterval
- `placeMarketOrder()` — executes immediately at current tick price, buys 10 shares
- `placeLimitOrder(triggerPrice)` — sets pending order, executes when price ≤ triggerPrice
- `placeStopLoss(triggerPrice)` — sets pending sell order, executes when price ≤ triggerPrice (sells all holdings)
- Each round allows exactly one order (orderRef guards against duplicates)
- Tick simulation checks pending orders at each new tick for limit/stop-loss triggers
- `startRound()` resets tick counter and starts playback; `nextRound()` advances to next round or completes sim
- On completion: remaining holdings auto-sold at final price, total P&L computed
- `visibleTicks` memoized for chart rendering (slice of stockData up to currentTick)
- `holdingsValue` computed live from holdings × currentPrice
- Score: grade based on ordersExecuted count + P&L positivity (S=3+positive, A=3, B=2, C=1, F=0)
- `lessonsLearned` collects targetLesson from all completed rounds
- Exposed: state, config, currentRoundData, currentTick, currentPrice, visibleTicks, holdingsValue, isPlaying, roundComplete, score, + all actions
